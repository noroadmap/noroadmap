No Road Map

This is my journal, started when I realized at 73 that if I didnt start paying attention, I would wake up old, unable to do the things I hoped to do. I realized that I had to have a plan, and recognize each day as important. The entries are about things that struck me. Some travel notes, random thoughts, projects, just life that I was there for. Ive saved it here for the people I care about so theyd have a sense of who I was, while I was here.
