June 22, 2025

Sunday, we checked out of the KOA and went over to Buddy’s for a group brunch.. we had about 30 people from Buddy’s past.. Gainesville, Damian’s, Atlanta.. over 50 years of good friends there for his 75th. His wife really went all out with all kinds of foods, cakes, rolls, etc to keep the group well fed.

Everyone sang Happy Birthday and Buddy was feted in style. Great to see him surrounded by so many friends from all over. Later folks brought out their guitars and had a nice sing along. We left to get back to Eustis.

This was our initial shakedown for the trailer.. and we had to adjust, pack, and load our for our trip to MN, WY, MT and SD. Leave Tuesday am and be back mid September. Its here!

Eric, Hal, Buddy
