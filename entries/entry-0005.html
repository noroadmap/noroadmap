June 16, 2025

Like a snowball, everything is coming quickly as we prepare for the 3 month trip up North and West.. I had made an appointment with the dealer for a full oil and fuel filter change, and inspection of the truck before putting about 7-8000 miles on her this summer. I also had the tires rotated and rebalanced. Funny how an oil/fuel filter change with a wash make it seem to run so much smoother. Her name (she agreed) is Betty.. Oh Black Betty was on.. and that was that, even though she is white. All my trucks end up with names.. my cars never do. Wonder why that is.

Making sure the equipment is maintained is also smoother on the road.. getting towed with a 26’ travel trailer behind you is expensive and no fun. The trailer is mostly packed except for clothes and personal items.. and ready for the shakedown to St Augustine this weekend. Come home Sunday night, load up whatever we forgot or dump what we thought we won’t use and leave Tuesday for Minnesota. I will be making lots of notes and photos on the trip so stay tuned. Meanwhile I have to fit in two scans, a crown fitting, the skin doctor, a haircut and breakfast with my cousin before Friday. Busy week.

Betty
