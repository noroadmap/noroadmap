I realized that I had to have a plan, and recognize each day as important. The entries are about things that struck me. Some travel notes, random thoughts, projects, just life.. that I was there for. I've saved it here for the people I care about — so they'd have a sense of who I was, while I was here.

Sloane and Grandpa
