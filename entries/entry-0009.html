June 20, 2025

Friday, a truly rugged emotional day. My cousin (82) had lived in a nice little springs area, by herself and getting along. The area has lots of wildlife like deers, bears, birds. Etc. and she loves they go by her back door and visit. Her daughter (60) lived in Atlanta by herself in a nice little house in a good area.. but when she would talk with her mom, who mentioned going to doctor appointments via Uber and some other difficulties getting around, felt that she needed to move down and take mo...

So, they bought a house, and moved in together and sold their respective homes. It has been a year, and now two ladies who have lived along for 20-30 years are learning that living together (especially with family) really takes a lot of effort. And now, they both feel they are doing more, the other is doing less, and will not try to meet in the middle.

So at breakfast with Jo and my cousin, I was asked to relay that she should be looking for another place to live. That has to be the most fearful thing to hear at her age.. and that there is no way she can afford/find a place to live in her little bubble, and will end up in a more “cityscape” apartment. I talked with both of them, and they are just not going to try to live together anymore, and it’s the other persons fault.

So that started the day off swimmingly! Was back and forth most of the day, then got my haircut super short for the trip.. finished packing for Saturdays trip and went to bed!
